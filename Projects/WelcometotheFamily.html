What is happening?

*This game is stil in development*

You are in an unknown house, every hour a new enemy happear, and each of them means an adjunctive layer of difficulty.
Find out how to survive each hour and discover why you are in that house.

Since I was in middle school, I dreamt about creating "my horror game", in fact, Ten years ago I decided to learn Scratch and create two of them. achieving that "objective" I felt unsatisfied because Scratch does not allow to create the standalone file of the game; I knew that I had to achieve a new objective.
In the last few months, I decided to hear the child version of me, giving birth to what I always dreamt about since I was a kid: a 3D standalone horror game! (Even if till today I didn't give it a name)
