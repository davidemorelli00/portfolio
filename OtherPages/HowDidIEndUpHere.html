How Did I End Up Here?

Pino, an italian night watchman, was sleeping on work untill a ghost wake him up and throw him in the castle that he was supposed to guard. Find a way to escape from the castle and go back to sleep!





How Did I End Up Here has been created in two weeks, in this game made with Unity Engine i had the chance to focus on creating one of my first basic enemy "I.A." system. Thanks to this project i even found out that i love to create Horror Games!
